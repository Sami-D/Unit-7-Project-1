BTS

Korea's first band to win at an American music award show. Debuting in 2013, BTS (also known as Bulletproof Boy Scouts or Beyond the Scene) is comprised of 7 members: Jin, Jimin, Jungkook, Taehyung (V), RapMonster, Suga and J-Hope. Led by Rap Monster, the band has 6 music videos with over 100 million views and a fan base that spans the globe (entitled 'Army').

Did you know?:

  • RapMonster is the main rapper and has the iq of a genius, ranking him in the top 1% of Korea.
  • Member Suga also goes by the name of Agust D and started producing his own music along with songs for other musicians.
  • Their band writes their own music and covers topics such as bullying, mental illness, and more.
